Overview
    Luck of the Sea is an enchantment to a fishing rod that increases luck while fishing.

Usage
    With the default loot tables, this enchantment increases the chance of "treasure" catches (by about 2% per level), while greatly lowering the chance of "junk" catches (by about 2% per level) and slightly lowering the chance of fish catches (by about 0.15% per level).
