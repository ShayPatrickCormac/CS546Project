Overview
    Cleaving is an enchantment applied to an axe that increases the axe's damage and shield disabling.

Usage
    Cleaving adds 1 + 1 × level [note 1] of damage and 0.5 seconds (10 game ticks) of shield stunning per level[note 2], for a maximum enchantment level of 3.

Incompatibilities
    Cleaving, Sharpness, Bane of Arthropods, and Smite are mutually exclusive. If two or more are combined by using commands, the effect of the enchants can stack.[needs testing]
