Overview
    A raw porkchop is a food item that can be eaten by the player or cooked to make a cooked porkchop.

Obtaining
  Mob loot
    Adult pigs drop 1–3 raw porkchop when killed. The maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 6 with Looting III. If killed while on fire, they drop cooked porkchop instead. Adult hoglins drop 2–4 raw porkchop when killed. The maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 7 with Looting III. If killed while on fire, they drop cooked porkchop instead.

Usage
  Food
    To eat raw porkchop, press and hold use while it is selected in the hotbar. Eating one restores 3 () hunger and 0.6 saturation.

  Smelting ingredient
    Name,Ingredients,Smelting recipe

  Trading
    In Bedrock Edition, novice-level butcher villagers have a 1⁄3 chance to buy 7 raw porkchop for an emerald as part of their trades. In Java Edition, novice-level butcher villagers have a 40% chance to buy 7 raw porkchop for an emerald.

  Wolves
    Raw porkchops can be used to breed and heal tamed wolves, lead them around, and make baby tamed wolves grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.

  Piglins
    Piglins instantly pick up raw or cooked porkchops that are within 1 block of them, unless they have already picked up one within the last 10 seconds. Porkchops picked up are not dropped upon the piglin's death.
