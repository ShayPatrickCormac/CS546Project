Overview
    Sugar is a food ingredient and brewing ingredient made from sugar canes.

Obtaining
  Mob loot
    Witches have a chance of dropping 0–2 sugar upon death. This is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 0–5 sugar.
