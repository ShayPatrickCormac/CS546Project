Overview
    Banner patterns are items used to customize banners inside looms.
There are six patterns in Java Edition and eight in Bedrock Edition.

Obtaining
  Crafting
    It is possible to craft 4 out of 6 banner patterns in Java Edition, or 6 out of 8 banner patterns in Bedrock Edition. They are crafted by combining one paper along with a certain material.

  Trading
    Globe banner pattern is obtained by trading with a master-level cartographer villager for a price of 8 emeralds.

  Chest loot
    Snout banner pattern is obtained in the Bastion Remnant as loot.

Usage
  Loom ingredient
    Banner patterns are used in looms to add customization to banners. The pattern must be combined with 1 banner and 1 dye. Upon usage in the loom, the banner pattern is not consumed.

    Name,Ingredients,Loom recipe,Description
    Flower Charge Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Flower,Emblazons a flower charge (flower icon)
    Creeper Charge Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Creeper,Emblazons a creeper charge (creeper face)
    Skull Charge Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Skull,Emblazons a skull charge (skull and crossbones)
    Thing Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Thing,Emblazons a thing (old Mojang logo)
    Snout Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Snout,Emblazons a piglin snout
    Globe Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Globe,Emblazons a globe (cube shaped earth)
    Bordure Indented Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Bordure,Emblazons a bordure indented (fancy border)‌ [ Bedrock Edition only ]
    Field Masoned Banner,Any Banner + Matching Dye,Field,Emblazons a field masoned (brick pattern)‌‌ [ Bedrock Edition only ]
