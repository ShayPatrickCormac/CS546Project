Overview
    Shulker shells are items dropped by shulkers that are used solely to craft shulker boxes.

Obtaining
  Mob loot
    In Bedrock Edition, shulkers drop 0-1 shulker shells. The maximum can be increased by 1 per level of Looting, which is 0-4 shells with Looting III. In Java Edition, shulkers have a 50% chance of dropping a shulker shell when killed. This is increased by 6.25% per level of Looting, for a maximum of 68.75% with Looting III.
